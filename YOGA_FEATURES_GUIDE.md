## What's New

### 1. **Animated Pose Previews** 
Every yoga card now displays a smooth, looping SVG animation showing the pose in action:
- Animations run continuously on the card
- Click the preview area to start the full pose session
- Each pose has a unique, anatomically-inspired animation

### 2. **Interactive Pose Modal**
When you tap "Start Pose", you get:
- **Large Animated Demonstration**: Full-screen SVG animation of the pose
- **Built-in Timer**: 
  - Play/Pause/Reset controls
  - Large, easy-to-read display
  - Perfect for holding poses
- **Step-by-Step Guide**: Numbered instructions with visual indicators
- **Benefits Highlight**: Green section showing all benefits
- **Caution Section**: Red warning for things to avoid

### 3. **Beautiful Visual Design**
- **Gradient Cards**: Warm, soothing colors that match MamaCare theme
- **Smooth Animations**: All interactions are fluid and responsive
- **Color Coding**:
  - 🌹 Rose/Pink: Main actions and highlights
  - 💚 Green: Benefits and positive info
  - 💙 Blue: Timer and breathing exercises
  - ❤️ Red: Cautions and warnings
- **Hover Effects**: Cards lift and glow when you hover over them

### 4. **Smart Filtering**
- Filter by trimester (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
- Filter by type (Breathing, Stretch, Strength, Cardio, Pelvic)

- Smooth transitions between filters

### 5. **Expandable Details**
- Click "Details" button to see full information
- Includes:
  - Why this pose is beneficial
  - Complete step-by-step instructions
  - All benefits listed

- Smooth expand/collapse animation

## How to Use

### Browsing Poses
1. Scroll through the yoga section
2. Each card shows:
   - Pose name
   - Duration and difficulty level
   - Animated preview
   - Short description

### Starting a Pose
**Option 1: Click "Start Pose" button**
- Opens full modal with timer and instructions

**Option 2: Tap the animated preview**
- Same as clicking "Start Pose"

### During a Pose
1. Watch the animated demonstration
2. Follow the numbered instructions
3. Use the timer:
   - Click "Start" to begin timing
   - Click "Pause" to pause
   - Click "Reset" to start over
4. Review benefits and cautions
5. Click "Done" when finished

### Learning More
1. Click "Details" button on any card
2. Expands to show:
   - Why this pose helps
   - Detailed step-by-step instructions
   - All benefits
   - Important cautions

## Tips for Best Experience

✅ **Do:**
- Practice poses regularly (daily if possible)
- Follow the step-by-step instructions carefully
- Use the timer to track your holds
- Read the cautions for your trimester
- Practice breathing exercises daily

❌ **Don't:**
- Force any pose beyond comfort
- Hold your breath during exercises
- Ignore pain signals
- Skip the warm-up
- Practice on a full stomach

## Trimester-Specific Recommendations

### 1st Trimester
- Focus on breathing exercises
- Gentle stretches
- Light strength building
- All poses are generally safe

### 2nd Trimester
- Increase strength training
- Pelvic floor focus
- Hip opening poses
- Avoid flat back positions

### 3rd Trimester
- Pelvic floor emphasis
- Labor preparation poses
- Breathing techniques
- Gentle movement only
