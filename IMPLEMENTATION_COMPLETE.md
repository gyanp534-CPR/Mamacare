## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Email Digest
- **DNS records take 24-48 hours** to propagate (set up early!)
- **Test thoroughly** before enabling for all users
- **Monitor bounce rate** — high bounces hurt deliverability
- **Respect unsubscribes** immediately (legal requirement)

### Demo Video
- **Mobile-first** — 70% of views will be on phone
- **Captions essential** — 85% watch without sound
- **First 3 seconds** determine if people keep watching

- **Time zones matter** — 12:01 AM PST = 12:31 PM IST
- **First hour critical** — rank algorithm weights early engagement
- **Be authentic** — community can smell fake enthusiasm
- **Respond to everyone** — even negative comments
