Pati / Partner ke liye
Pregnancy Partner Guide 💑
🤱

Aap ki bhoomika bahut important hai

Pregnancy sirf maa ki journey nahi hai — partner ke support se yeh safar kitna alag ho jaata hai. Yahan sab kuch hai jo aapko jaanna chahiye.

Trimester-wise Support
Har phase mein kya karein?

🌱 Pehli Trimester (1–13 weeks)

  • Nausea ke time saath raho
  • Doctor appointments attend karo
  • Fatigue ko seriously lo
  • Diet mein help karo
  • Emotional mood swings ke liye patience rakho
  • Khaana banana sikho / order karo

🌸 Doosri Trimester (14–27)

  • Baby ka naam sochne mein participate karo
  • Nursery setup mein madad karo
  • Baby movements feel karo
  • Prenatal classes attend karo
  • Back massage regularly do
  • Hospital bag planning mein help karo

🌟 Teesri Trimester (28–40)

  • Birth plan discuss karo milke
  • Hospital route practice karo
  • Work leave plan karo
  • Contraction timer sikhо
  • Raat ko pani / snacks laane ke liye ready raho
  • Delivery room mein saath rehne ki taiyaari karo
Dos & Don'ts
Kya karein, kya na karein?

✅ Zaroor Karein

  • Feelings validate karo — "haan, yeh normal hai"
  • Physically present raho, especially raat ko
  • Ghar ke kaam mein zyada hissa lo
  • Doctor ki baatein dhyan se suno
  • Appreciation dikhao — chota gift, surprise dinner
  • Mood swing pe patience rakho
  • Baby ke baare mein curious raho aur questions poocho

❌ Bilkul Mat Karein

  • "Itna drama kyu kar rahi ho" kabhi mat kaho
  • Pain ko dismiss mat karo
  • Apni problems zyada bade mat batao
  • Doctor appointments skip mat karo
  • Body changes pe negative comment mat karo
  • Phone pe busy rehna during quality time
  • Labour mein hospital mein alone rehna

Important Reminder
Aapki Mental Health bhi Matter Karti Hai 💙

Partners mein bhi prenatal anxiety hoti hai — yeh normal hai. Agar aap anxious, scared ya overwhelmed feel kar rahe hain toh apne partner se baat karein ya doctor se milein. Dono ki wellbeing se hi ek healthy family banti hai. 🌟
