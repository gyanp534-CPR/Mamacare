## 🌟 What's New

### ⏱️ **Contraction Timer** (Major Feature)

Track your labor contractions with confidence! The new Contraction Timer helps you know exactly when it's time to go to the hospital.

**Features:**
- ⏱️ **Real-Time Timer** — Precise MM:SS display with smooth counting
- 📊 **Automatic Statistics** — Duration, frequency, and count tracking
- 🚨 **5-1-1 Rule Detection** — Smart alerts when contractions match labor pattern
- 📝 **Complete History** — All contractions saved and organized by date
- 📤 **CSV Export** — Share your data with your doctor
- 📱 **Share Summary** — Quick share via WhatsApp, SMS, or clipboard
- 📚 **Labor Stages Guide** — Educational content about Early, Active, and Transition phases
- ⚠️ **Warning Signs** — Know when to call your doctor immediately

**User Benefits:**
- Know when to go to hospital (no guesswork!)
- Track pattern over time
- Share accurate data with medical team
- Feel prepared and confident
- Reduce anxiety during labor

## 🔒 Security Enhancements (Deployed)

All critical security vulnerabilities have been fixed:

1. ✅ **XSS Protection** — DOMPurify sanitization prevents script injection
2. ✅ **Authentication Security** — JWT verification on all API endpoints
3. ✅ **Rate Limiting** — Database-backed persistent rate limiting (no bypasses)
4. ✅ **CORS Security** — Domain-locked, no wildcard access
5. ✅ **Security Headers** — Full CSP, X-Frame-Options, and more

**Impact:** Your data and privacy are now fully protected.

## 🐛 Known Issues

### Minor Issues
1. **Safari Vibration** — Safari doesn't support vibration API (not fixable)
2. **Offline 5-1-1 Alerts** — Notifications may not work offline (browser limitation)
3. **LocalStorage Limit** — Journal photos still use base64 (fix coming in v8.2)

### Workarounds
- Safari users: Enable notification sound for audio alert
- Offline users: Timer still works, just no push notifications
- Photo users: Limit to 5-10 photos until v8.2

**None of these are blockers for production use.**
