# 📸 How to Generate 10 Sample Cards for Social Media

## Quick Instructions

1. Open **Mama Gyan** in your browser
2. Navigate to **📸 Share Cards** tab
3. For each card below, follow these steps:
   - Select the **Theme** (click theme button)
   - Enter **Week Number**
   - Type **Emoji** in emoji field
   - Paste **Message** in text area
   - Click **"✨ Generate Card"**
   - Click **"⬇️ Download"**
   - Save with filename: `mama-gyan-week-XX-themename.png`
