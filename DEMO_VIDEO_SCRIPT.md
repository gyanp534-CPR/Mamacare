## 📱 Screen Recording Tips

### Setup
1. **Device:** iPhone 13/14 or Android flagship
2. **Orientation:** Portrait (vertical video)
3. **Resolution:** 1080x1920 (9:16 for Instagram/TikTok)
4. **Frame rate:** 60fps for smooth animations

### Recording Settings
- **Clear cache** before recording
- **Disable notifications** (DND mode)
- **Full battery** or plugged in
- **Good lighting** (natural daylight preferred)
- **Stable hands** (use tripod or phone stand)

### Screen Recording Apps
- iOS: Built-in Screen Recording (Control Center)
- Android: AZ Screen Recorder (no watermark)
- Alternative: Record from Mac with QuickTime

### Demo Data
Create sample profile:
- Name: Priya Sharma
- Due date: 4 weeks from today
- Weight logs: Last 4 weeks
- Mood logs: Last week
- 1 upcoming appointment
- 3 medicines active

## 🎙️ Voiceover Guidelines

### Voice Characteristics
- **Female voice** (relatable to target audience)
- **Age:** 25-35 years old
- **Tone:** Warm, friendly, confident
- **Accent:** Neutral Indian English (not too heavy)
- **Pace:** Medium (not rushed)

### Recording Setup
- **Microphone:** USB condenser mic (Blue Yeti or similar)
- **Room:** Quiet room with soft furnishings (absorbs echo)
- **Position:** 6-8 inches from mic
- **Pop filter:** Essential for clear audio

### Voiceover Tools
- **Audacity** (free, open-source)
- **Adobe Audition** (professional)
- **AI voice:** ElevenLabs (if budget allows)
