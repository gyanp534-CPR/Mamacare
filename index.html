Safe Exercise
Prenatal Yoga & Exercise 🧘
⚠️ Avoid Karein
Pregnancy mein kya nahi karna chahiye
  • Flat back pe litnaa (pehli trimester ke baad) — uterus main artery par pressure
  • Intense ab crunches / sit-ups
  • High-impact sports — skiing, contact sports, horse riding
  • Hot yoga ya sauna — overheating baby ke liye harmful
  • Heavy lifting (>10kg) without doctor's clearance
  • Koi bhi exercise jo dard ya dizziness de
  • Agle 24 ghante mein exercise na karo agar spotting ho
