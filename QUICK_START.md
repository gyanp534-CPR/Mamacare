# 🧘‍♀️ Yoga Section - Quick Start Guide

## What Changed?

Your yoga section now has **animated pose demonstrations** instead of static text. Each pose shows a smooth, looping animation that demonstrates the movement.

## How to Use

### 1. Browse Poses
Scroll through the yoga section. Each card shows:
- Animated pose preview (watch it move!)
- Pose name
- Duration and difficulty
- Quick description

### 2. Start a Pose
**Two ways:**
- Click the **"Start Pose"** button
- Tap the animated preview area

### 3. Follow Along
In the modal you'll see:
- **Large animated demonstration** at the top
- **Step-by-step instructions** (numbered)
- **Built-in timer** (Play/Pause/Reset)
- **Benefits** (green section)
- **Cautions** (red section)

### 4. Use the Timer
- Click **"Start"** to begin timing
- Click **"Pause"** to pause
- Click **"Reset"** to start over
- Timer shows minutes:seconds

### 5. Learn More
Click **"Details"** on any card to see:
- Why this pose helps
- Complete instructions
- All benefits
- Important cautions

## Troubleshooting

### Animation not showing?
- Refresh the page
- Check your browser (use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled

### Timer not working?
- Click "Start" button
- Check if timer is counting
- Click "Reset" to restart

### Modal not opening?
- Click "Start Pose" button
- Or tap the preview area
- Check browser console for errors

### Animations stuttering?
- Close other tabs
- Reduce browser extensions
- Try a different browser
- Restart your device
